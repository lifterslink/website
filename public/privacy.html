Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Bia ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Bia mobile application (the "App") and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using Bia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Name (from Apple Sign In)
  • Email address (from Apple Sign In)
  • Username (you create this during onboarding)
  • Optional profile information (bio, profile photo, coach status)

Athlete Profile Claims:

  • If you claim an OpenPowerlifting athlete profile, we store the association between your account and that athlete ID
  • This links your Bia account to publicly available competition results

Coach-Athlete Relationships:

  • Coach connections you create
  • Notes about coaching relationships
  • Start dates of coaching relationships

Social Connections:

  • Users you follow
  • Users who follow you

2. Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers

Usage Information:

  • Features you use
  • Search queries
  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent in the app

Performance Data:

  • Crash reports
  • Error logs
  • App performance metrics

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Precise geolocation (GPS coordinates)
  • Health or medical information
  • Financial information (the app is free)
  • Biometric data
  • Contacts from your device
  • Photos from your device (unless you explicitly upload one)

How We Use Your Information

Provide Core Functionality

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Display your athlete profile
  • Connect you with coaches and other athletes
  • Show your competition results
  • Enable social features (following, network feed)
  • Search and discover other athletes

Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Debug technical issues
  • Optimize app performance
  • Develop new features

Communicate With You

  • Send important account notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you of app updates (if you opt in)

Ensure Security

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect user safety

Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Data:

  • Your account data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • API requests are processed through Modal (serverless platform)
  • All data is stored in the United States

How We Protect Data:

  • Industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure backend infrastructure

Local Storage:

  • Some data is cached on your device for performance
  • Cached data includes athlete profiles and search results
  • Cache can be cleared by uninstalling the app

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to other Bia users:

  • Your username
  • Your bio (if you add one)
  • Your claimed athlete profile (if you claim one)
  • Your competition results (from OpenPowerlifting)
  • Your coach status (if applicable)
  • Your following/follower counts

Third-Party Services We Use

Apple Sign In:

  • Used for secure authentication
  • We receive your name and email (or private relay email)
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Supabase (Database Hosting):

  • Stores your account and profile data
  • Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy
  • Located in United States

Modal (API Platform):

  • Processes API requests
  • Does not store personal data
  • Subject to Modal's Privacy Policy

OpenPowerlifting (Competition Data):

  • Publicly available powerlifting database
  • We display this data but do not own it
  • Subject to OpenPowerlifting's policies

When We Must Disclose Information

We may disclose your information if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • Necessary to protect user safety
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice)

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view and edit your profile information in the app at any time.

Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your account by:

  1. Going to Settings > Delete Account in the app, or
  2. Emailing us at privacy@buildingbia.com

What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your account and profile information are permanently deleted
  • Your social connections (following/followers) are removed
  • Cached data on your device is cleared
  • This action cannot be undone

What is NOT deleted:

  • OpenPowerlifting competition data (publicly available)
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

Export Your Data

You can request a copy of your data by emailing privacy@buildingbia.com. We will provide:

  • Your profile information
  • Your social connections
  • Your coach relationships
  • Format: JSON or CSV

Opt Out of Communications

  • You can disable push notifications in your device settings
  • You cannot opt out of critical account security notifications

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of the sale of your information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Contact privacy@buildingbia.com to exercise these rights.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Contact privacy@buildingbia.com to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Bia is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@buildingbia.com and we will delete it.

Data Retention

Active Accounts:

  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • Cached data on your device expires automatically (typically 24 hours)

Deleted Accounts:

  • Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
  • Backup systems may retain data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

Competition Data:

  • OpenPowerlifting data is retained indefinitely (it's publicly available historical records)

International Data Transfers

If you access Bia from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer.

We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Mobile App:

The app does not use cookies. We collect usage data through:

  • Standard app analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • Performance monitoring

Website:

Our website (buildingbia.com) uses:

  • Essential cookies for site functionality
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) - you can opt out via browser settings

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

For material changes, we will:

  • Notify you via email (if you provided one)
  • Display a prominent notice in the app
  • Require your consent for continued use (if legally required)

Your continued use of Bia after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@buildingbia.com
Support: support@buildingbia.com
Website: https://buildingbia.com/privacy

Your Consent

By using Bia, you consent to:

  • This Privacy Policy
  • The collection and use of information as described
  • The transfer of data to the United States (if you're international)

Open Source

Security Incidents

If we experience a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours (as required by law)
  • Explain what data was affected
  • Detail steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Provide guidance on protecting yourself
