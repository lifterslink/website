Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bia?
Bia is the global community for powerlifters. It's a platform where athletes can track their competition history, connect with coaches, discover other lifters, and stay engaged with the powerlifting community. We integrate data from OpenPowerlifting to provide comprehensive competition records and athlete profiles.
How do I create an account?
Creating an account is easy! Download the Bia app from the App Store, then sign in with Apple Sign In. You'll create a username and can optionally add profile information like a bio and profile photo. If you've competed in powerlifting meets, you can claim your athlete profile to link your competition history.
How do I claim my athlete profile?
After creating your account, you can search for your name in the athlete database. When you find your profile from OpenPowerlifting, tap "Claim Profile" and follow the verification steps. Once verified, your competition history will be linked to your Bia account and visible on your profile.
Is Bia free to use?
Yes! Bia is completely free to use. There are no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or hidden fees. We believe in making powerlifting more accessible and connected for everyone.
Where does the competition data come from?
All competition data in Bia comes from OpenPowerlifting, a nonprofit organization dedicated to creating a permanent, open archive of powerlifting history. They maintain the most comprehensive database of powerlifting meets and results worldwide.
What features are available?
Current features include:
  • View your complete competition history
  • Search and discover other powerlifters
  • Follow athletes and build your network
  • Connect with coaches and manage coaching relationships
  • Browse meet results and federation records
  • Track your personal records and progress
We're constantly building new features, so stay tuned!
How do I connect with a coach?
You can search for coaches in the app and send them a connection request. Once they accept, they'll be listed as your coach and can view your competition history. Coaches can also manage multiple athletes and track their progress.
Can I use Bia if I haven't competed yet?
Absolutely! Bia is for all powerlifters, whether you're a seasoned competitor or just getting started. You can create an account, follow athletes, connect with coaches, and be part of the community. When you compete in your first meet, you'll be able to claim your profile and see your results.
My competition results are missing or incorrect. What do I do?
Since our data comes from OpenPowerlifting, any corrections or additions need to be submitted there. Visit openpowerlifting.org and use their contact form to report issues. Once they update their database, the changes will automatically appear in Bia.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account at any time by going to Settings â†’ Delete Account in the app. This will permanently remove your profile, connections, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone. Alternatively, you can email us at support@buildingbia.com to request account deletion.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
What platforms is Bia available on?
Bia is currently available on iOS via the App Store. Android support is coming soon! Join our waitlist on the homepage to be notified when we launch on Android.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
We appreciate bug reports! Please email us at support@buildingbia.com with:
  • A description of the bug
  • Steps to reproduce it
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
  • Your device model and iOS version
We'll investigate and fix it as soon as possible.
Can I suggest a new feature?
Absolutely! We'd love to hear your ideas. Email us at support@buildingbia.com with your feature request. We read every suggestion and consider them when planning our roadmap.
How can I stay updated on new features?
Follow us on Instagram @buildingbia for regular updates, feature announcements, and behind-the-scenes looks at what we're building. You can also join our waitlist on the homepage to receive email updates.
