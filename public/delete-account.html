We're sorry to see you go. Click below to send a deletion request.

⚠️ This action is permanent

  • Your username and profile will be deleted
  • All social connections (followers, following) will be removed
  • Your coach relationships will be deleted
  • Claimed athlete profiles will be unclaimed
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion

ℹ️ What stays public

Your competition history from OpenPowerlifting will remain publicly available, as this is historical competition data that exists independently of your Bia account.

How it works:

  1. Click the button below to open your email app
  2. The email will be pre-filled with your deletion request
  3. Add your email/Apple ID and username (if you remember it)
  4. Send the email
  5. We'll process your request within 7 business days
  6. You'll receive a confirmation email when complete
