Welcome to Lifters Link; a platform made for and by powerlifters

We're building infrastructure as strong as the sport.

Lifters Link is a comprehensive platform for the global powerlifting community where athletes, coaches, and meet directors can connect, spectate, showcase a history of their performance, and organize, register for, and execute meets.

Like any good lift needing a warm up, we’re testing some ideas. Based on dozens of customer interviews, live meet notifications emerged as the most pressing concern. We’ve selected a few meets over the coming weeks to validate these findings.
